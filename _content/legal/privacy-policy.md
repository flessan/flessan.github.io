**Last Updated: 2025**

This privacy policy ("Policy") describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our website.

### 1. Information We Collect

We believe in minimalism, not just in design, but also in data collection. Here’s what we collect and why:

*   **Theme Preference**: We use your browser's `localStorage` to remember your light or dark mode preference. This information stays entirely on your device and is not transmitted to us.
*   **Analytics (No-Cookie)**: We may use a privacy-friendly analytics service that does not use cookies and anonymizes your IP address. This helps us understand website traffic (e.g., which pages are popular) without identifying you personally. We do not collect any personal data through analytics.

We do **not** use tracking cookies or collect any other personally identifiable information.

### 2. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely for:

*   **Improving User Experience**: Remembering your theme preference.
*   **Website Improvement**: Understanding general traffic patterns to improve our content and layout.

### 3. Data Storage and Security

Your theme preference is stored in your browser's `localStorage` and is not accessible by us. Any anonymized analytics data is stored securely by our third-party analytics provider.

### 4. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, your privacy is inherently protected. You can clear your theme preference by clearing your browser's cache and `localStorage`.

### 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Policy on this page.
