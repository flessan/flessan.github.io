## 📋 Project Overview

**List2do++** is a powerful, modern **task management Progressive Web Application (PWA)** built to help users stay productive and organized.  
It combines a sleek design with full offline support — perfect for managing daily tasks across devices.  
Whether online or offline, List2do++ keeps your productivity flowing effortlessly.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🔧 Core Functionality
- **Add Tasks**: Create tasks instantly with a simple interface  
- **Sorting Options**: Organize tasks by priority, date, or category  
- **Edit & Update**: Modify tasks seamlessly in real-time  
- **Delete Management**: Remove completed or unwanted tasks safely  

### 📱 Progressive Web App (PWA) Support
- **Offline Ready**: Works fully without an internet connection  
- **Installable App**: Add to home screen for native-like experience  
- **Cross-Platform**: Runs on desktop, mobile, and tablets  
- **Fast Loading**: Optimized with Service Worker caching  

### 🎨 Customization & UI
- **Custom Themes**: Personalize your workspace  
- **Dark / Light Mode**: Switch for visual comfort  
- **Responsive Layout**: Perfectly fits any screen size  
- **User Preferences**: Saved locally for persistent settings  

### 📊 Analytics & Statistics
- **Task Insights**: Analyze task completion and progress  
- **Progress Charts**: Visual feedback with dynamic graphs  
- **Time Tracking**: See how time is spent on each task  
- **Productivity Overview**: Clear, easy-to-read summaries  

### 🔗 Quick Access Shortcuts
Boost your productivity with one-click shortcuts:
- **📅 Calendar**: Instantly check your schedule  
- **🎵 Spotify**: Launch focus playlists  
- **👨‍💻 GitHub**: Access development resources

### 💻 PWA Installation
1. Open the app in your preferred browser  
2. Look for the **Install** prompt or use browser menu  
3. Click **Install** to add List2do++ to your home screen  
4. Enjoy the native-like offline experience
